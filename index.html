Cognitive Skills

I have developed strong analytical skills through laboratory investigations and case-study-based assessments. My ability to identify and formulate engineering problems is demonstrated in the EV Conversion Training Vehicle project, where I contributed to problem scoping, literature review, and analysis of technical constraints.

Technical Skills

My CAD competency in SolidWorks and AutoCAD demonstrates my ability to design components and systems. The EV conversion project required iterative design and development, where I contributed to the mechanical layout, component sizing, and integration planning.

Cognitive Skills

Experimental investigations conducted in Materials Testing and Dynamics labs demonstrate my ability to design experiments, collect data, and draw conclusions. I consistently presented data with appropriate analysis tools including graphs, regression analysis, and statistical evaluation.

Technical Skills

Professional Skills

My understanding of the engineer's role in society is demonstrated through coursework addressing health, safety, and cultural considerations in engineering design. The EV conversion project specifically required consideration of road safety standards and national EV adoption policy.

PO7 — Environment & Sustainability

Professional Skills

Sustainability is a core theme in my engineering studies. The EV Conversion Training Vehicle project is inherently aligned with sustainable transport goals, and I have evaluated the lifecycle environmental impacts of engineering decisions in relevant assignments.

PO8 — Ethics

Professional Skills

I am committed to professional ethics both academically and in my business operations. Through PES membership and coursework on engineering ethics, I have developed an understanding of the responsibilities that come with being a professional engineer.

PO9 — Communication

My communication skills are evidenced through comprehensive technical reports, project presentations, business documentation for Tweedy Essentials, and event coordination for the Taylor's Symphony Orchestra. I consistently receive strong feedback on written and verbal communication.

PO10 — Individual & Teamwork

I function effectively both independently and within teams. The EV Conversion group project (PRJ63204) demonstrates my ability to collaborate in a multidisciplinary team, while Tweedy Essentials demonstrates my capacity to work independently as an entrepreneur.

PO11 — Project Management

PO12 — Lifelong Learning
